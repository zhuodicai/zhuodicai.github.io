Virtualando!: Real-Time Social Space



Category     Duration     Tool / Material    
Real-Time Communications; Design; Development 2022 @ New York University JavaScript

Virtualando! is for strangers who love road trip. Especially for the ones who drive alone and hope to share beautiful scenes and moments!

Surfing on the Internet is just like driving on the road. In Virtualando!, you can stay with each other silently and anonymously with the jazz music on and scenery passing by. Or, if you want to build connections, try the interactions! To break the ice, start with the car horn!

> operation
>> move mouse - change car position
>> ⬇️ - open window
>> ⬆️ - close window
>> press touchpad / click - honk car horn
