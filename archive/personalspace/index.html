Personal Space


Category     Duration     Tool / Material    
Installation 2020 C++; Arduino; Pressure Sensor; Buzzer; Vellum Translucent Paper; Frosted PVC Plastic Sheet; Transparent String

People in intimate relationships still need their own personal space, which provides an opportunity for individuals to adjust their social roles. lf they are constantly interfered during the period, it seems to enhance the interaction and shorten the distance, but in fact, it deprives their opportunity to buffer, thus causing a sense of suffocating.

The device materializes the personal space in the intimate relationship, simulates the above interaction process with the control feedback system, and appeals to the people in the relationship to respect each other's space.
