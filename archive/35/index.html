Digital Investigation Report: Chinese 35-Year-Old Crisis


Category     Duration     Tool / Material    
Data; Design; Report April 2022 - May 2022 @ New York University Python; Figma


This is a digital investigation based on research and data crawling with Python; all data sources have been reviewed to ensure data reliability, they come from authorities, companies with top market share in the segment, and organizations affiliated with authorities or top companies.

Young people in China often discuss the 35-year-old crisis, and in recent years we are also concerned about the demographic dividend. Even with the enactment of preferential policies, the marriage and fertility rates continue to decline. What is happening and what is the relationship between 35 crisis and this problem?

This investigation is based on Chinese social context, aiming to answer the question of whether low marriage and fertility rates are related to the 35 crisis.
