Depression, Social Media


Category     Duration     Tool / Material    
Installation 2019 C++; Arduino; Excel; Laser Cutter; Switch Button; Pump; White Eustoma; Silicone Tubing; Acrylic Sheet; Beaker; Edible Paint

People with depressive disorder are more dependent on the Internet for social life, and Internet users play an important role. When the Internet users convey negative comments to patients, they will gradually accumulate and aggravate the symptoms of patients. When the Internet users choose to provide support and care, it can relieve the mood of patients and reduce the risk of suicide and death.

This device presents the victims in the form of life, narrates the emotional distance between the participants and the victims, and arouses people's attention and care to the depression patients in social media.
