Fact


Category     Duration     Tool / Material    
Installation 2020 Python; Welding; Lens; Camera Module; Metal Rod

The way information communicators express themselves affects people's perception of the fact. In order to leave enough space for explanation and avoid criticism, communicators often use expressions that emphasize or weaken the truth. Emphasis refers to the selective use of part of the truth, while weakening adds more information based on the truth to reduce the weight and presence of the fact. 

The dynamic presentation of fact, emphasized and weakened, prompts the viewer to consider the connection between such expressions and lies.
